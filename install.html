Getting agrade running

Agrade is safe to run. Here's how to get past the Windows warning in three steps.

01

Download the installer

Click the download button on the home page and save the file. Your browser may warn you that the file is not commonly downloaded. Click Keep to save it.

02

Click "More info" on the blue screen

When you run the installer, Windows will show a blue screen saying "Windows protected your PC". Click More info at the bottom of that screen.

Windows protected your PC, then More info

03

Click "Run anyway"

After clicking More info, a Run anyway button will appear. Click it and the installer will proceed normally.

Run anyway

04

Sign in when the app opens

agrade will open your browser to the login page automatically. Sign in or create an account and the app will be ready to use. Use Ctrl H to hide it and Ctrl B to bring it back.
